# Radio, microwave, THz

> **Mechanism:** Electromagnetic wave reception

## Detectors

- **Antenna + rectifier** — Classical RF reception; the antenna captures the field, a diode rectifier demodulates.
- **Crystal diode** — Point-contact diode demodulator. The original "cat's-whisker" AM radio detector.
- **Microwave bolometer** — Thermal RF detector; absorbed power heats a sensitive resistor whose change is measured.
- **SIS junction** — Superconductor-insulator-superconductor tunnel junction; a quantum mixer used in mm-wave and submm radio astronomy (e.g. ALMA receivers).
- **HEMT mixer** — High-electron-mobility transistor; low-noise mixing across microwave bands. The front-end amplifier in modern radio telescopes.
