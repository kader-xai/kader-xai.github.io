# Acceleration

> **Mechanism:** Newton's second law on a proof mass

## Detectors

- **MEMS capacitive** — Silicon proof mass on flexible tethers; displacement changes a capacitance gap. The accelerometer in every phone.
- **Piezoelectric** — Charge develops across a piezoelectric crystal (quartz, PZT) under stress. AC-coupled — cannot measure constant acceleration.
- **Piezoresistive** — Bonded or implanted resistor whose resistance changes with strain. DC-capable but temperature sensitive.
- **Servo / force-balance** — Closed-loop nulling: a feedback coil pushes the proof mass back to rest; the drive current is the output. Highest-accuracy class.
