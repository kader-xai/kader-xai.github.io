# Ultraviolet

> **Mechanism:** High-energy photon detection

## Detectors

- **SiC photodiode** — Silicon carbide; wide-bandgap, solar-blind (rejects visible). Used for flame detection.
- **GaN photodiode** — Gallium nitride; deep-UV (200–365 nm) semiconductor detector.
- **UV PMT** — PMT with a bialkali or solar-blind photocathode; single-photon UV counting.
- **Phototube** — Simple vacuum diode with a photocathode (no dynodes). Historical UV detector.
