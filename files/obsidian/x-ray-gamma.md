# X-ray & gamma ray

> **Mechanism:** Ionizing photon interaction

## Detectors

- **Scintillator + PMT** — A high-Z crystal (NaI, BGO, LYSO) converts a γ-photon into visible photons; a PMT reads them. Backbone of nuclear medicine and security imaging.
- **HPGe detector** — High-purity germanium semiconductor; the gold standard for γ-ray spectroscopy (~0.2 % energy resolution at 1 MeV). Liquid-nitrogen cooled.
- **CdTe / CZT** — Cadmium telluride or cadmium-zinc-telluride; room-temperature γ semiconductor. Compact imaging without cryogenics.
- **Geiger–Müller tube** — Gas-filled tube; an ionising photon triggers an avalanche that is counted. No energy information — just counts.
- **Image intensifier** — Photocathode → micro-channel plate → phosphor screen. Converts low-light, UV, or X-ray into a viewable image. The optics behind night vision.
