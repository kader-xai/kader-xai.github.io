📖 How every set works (read once)

1 · RIR (Reps In Reserve)

RIR 2 = stop with 2 clean reps left. Compounds RIR 1–3, isolations RIR 0–1.

2 · Warm-up ≠ working sets

Do 2–3 ramp-up sets before the first heavy lift. They don't count toward the listed working sets.

3 · Double progression

Same weight until you hit the top of the rep range on every set, then add the smallest jump.

4 · Tempo

Lower ~2 s under control, no bouncing, drive up with intent.

5 · Rest

Big lifts 2–3 min, machines/isolation 60–90 s.

6 · Deload (Week 6)

Drop 1 set, ~70% weight, RIR 3+. Come back stronger.
